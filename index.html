Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most common questions about DevConf 2026.

What is included in my conference ticket?

Your ticket includes access to all keynote sessions, technical talks, workshops, networking events, lunch, coffee breaks, and conference materials.

Can I transfer or cancel my ticket?

Yes. Tickets can be transferred to another attendee or canceled for a full refund up to 30 days before the event.

Will the sessions be recorded?

Yes. All conference sessions will be recorded. Standard ticket holders receive 30 days of access, while Pro attendees receive lifetime access.

Is food and parking available?

Yes. Complimentary lunch, snacks, coffee, and beverages are provided each day. Paid parking is available near the venue.

Who should attend DevConf 2026?

DevConf is designed for software engineers, web developers, mobile developers, DevOps engineers, AI practitioners, students, and tech enthusiasts of all experience levels.
